Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

My Universe Diary ("the App") is a personal diary app developed by PHAM VAN DAT. Your privacy matters deeply to us. This policy explains clearly what data the App handles and how.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data with us or any third party. This includes:

  • Diary entries and text
  • Photos attached to entries
  • Tags and dates
  • Your name or profile information

2. Local Storage Only

All data you create in the App — diary entries, photos, settings — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and the device's local file system. We have zero access to this data.

3. Photos & Camera

The App may request access to your Photo Library to let you attach photos to diary entries. These photos are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded anywhere.

4. No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any of the following:

  • Analytics or tracking SDKs
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services that transmit data
  • Social media SDKs

5. Data Deletion

You can delete any diary entry at any time from within the App. Deleting the App from your device permanently removes all associated data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, the App is safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
