Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

My Universe Diary ("the App") is a personal diary app developed by PHAM VAN DAT. Your privacy matters deeply to us. This policy explains clearly what data the App handles and how.

1. Your Diary Data

All diary entries, photos, tags, and settings you create in the App are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and local file system. We have zero access to your diary content.

  • Diary entries and text
  • Photos attached to entries
  • Tags and dates
  • Your name or profile information

2. Analytics & Crash Reporting

The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics (provided by Google) to help us understand how the App is used and to fix crashes. These services may collect:

  • Anonymous usage events (e.g. entry created, screen viewed)
  • Crash logs and device information (iOS version, device model)
  • App session information

This data is anonymous and does not include your diary content, name, or any personally identifiable information. For more details, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

3. Photos & Camera

The App may request access to your Photo Library to let you attach photos to diary entries. These photos are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers.

4. Advertising

The App does not display ads and does not share data with advertising networks.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete any diary entry at any time from within the App. Deleting the App from your device permanently removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
